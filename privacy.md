Privacy Policy for The Shabbat App

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy outlines how "The Shabbat App" handles your information.

2. Information We Collect
To provide a personalized and serene experience, we collect:

Name: Used solely for the "Dedication" and "Baruch HaShem" greeting features.

Zip Code: Used to calculate accurate Shabbat candle lighting and Havdalah times for your specific location.

No GPS Tracking: We do not track your real-time GPS location; we only use the Zip Code you provide.

3. Subscriptions and Payments
All financial transactions for the $2.99/month subscription are handled securely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not see or store your credit card information.

4. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data stays within the app to help you prepare for the Holy Shabbat.

5. Contact Us
If you have questions, please contact us via our GitHub support page.
